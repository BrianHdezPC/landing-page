The Studio Apartments

At Santa Marta Apartaestudios you will find the ideal place to stay during your visit, thanks to a mixture of excellent customer care, good services and quality facilities at great rates.

We have comfortable and furnished studio apartments available in an excellent location near Universidad del Magdalena, which gives you close access to restaurants, pharmacies, entertainment and other commercial areas of the university sector. Another plus is that we are 15 minutes away from the Historic city center or its most important Shopping Malls.
